Here are some simple scientific facts about how habits are acquired through repetition:

  • The 21/90 rule: It takes 21 days to build a habit and 90 days to make it a permanent lifestyle change.
  • Habit stacking: The best way to form a new habit is to tie it to an existing one.
  • Start small: Don't try to make too many changes at once. Start with small, manageable habits and build from there.
